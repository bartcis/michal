About

My name is Michal Cis. I am professional seismic Party Chief and Project Manager.
I graduated from University of Science and Technology in Cracow on faculty Prospecting Geophysics with the top of the class final result.
I work in Seismic Industry since 2003 and continualsy improve my skills. My in-depth understanding of on-shore seismic operations comes from gradual promotion from Junior Observer to Seismic Party Chief.
Personally I am happy husband and father of three children.
I am experienced with running seismic operations in deserts, plains, mountains and urban terrains. I have worked in 8 different countries so far.
Throughout my career I have completed 23 Vibroseis, 1 Explosive and 11 combined Vibroseis-Explosive projects.
I hold various qualifications and certificates that prove my expertise. These are some of them:
- Managing Director of companies using drilling and blasting metod as exploitation technique.
- High Grade of geophysical survey expertise using explosives.
- Grade IX of geological qualifications.
